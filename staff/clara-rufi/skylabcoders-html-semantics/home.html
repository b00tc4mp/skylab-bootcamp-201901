Who we are

SKYLAB is an initiative of a network of professionals with more than 20 years of experience in the online sector. Our Dean, David Monreal has worked for 12 years in InfoJobs with business and product development responsibilities. He has the support of a Board of Counselors made up of professionals with over 25 years in the industry to ensure that our training programs are tailored to the needs of the market.
